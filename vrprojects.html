Knight VR

Knight is the brand name for all of my VR projects.

I have been experimenting with VR for about a year and have spent most of that time programming in Unity with the Oculus Quest. However, I have also recently started experimenting with the A-Frame libraries for viewing VR scenes in the browser.

Game demos

Water VR

Experimentation with Water simulation in resource contrained VR applications (e.g. Oculus Quest). See 'SideQuest' for details on installing and running the app.

Depth VR

Experimentation with using information from depth cameras in VR. The application uses the Intel ReleaSense D435i to stream data directly to the Oculus Quest via the USB port.
